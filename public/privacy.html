Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

AnimeBoxD collects only the information needed to run the app and keep each user's library connected to their account.

Information we collect

When you create an account, AnimeBoxD may store your email address, username, avatar, bio, anime and manga library entries, ratings, notes, favorites, settings, reports, and related app activity.

How we use information

We use this information to sign you in, save your library, personalize your profile, respond to reports, improve the app, and protect the service.

Third-party services

AnimeBoxD may use Supabase for authentication and database storage, Netlify for hosting, Resend for account emails, and public anime data APIs for anime and manga information.

Advertising and analytics

AnimeBoxD may add analytics or advertising in the future. If that happens, this policy will be updated before those services are used publicly.

Your choices

You can contact us to ask about your data, report a concern, or request account-related help.
