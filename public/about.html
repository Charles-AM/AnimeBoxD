About AnimeBoxD

AnimeBoxD is a personal diary for anime and manga fans. It helps viewers keep track of what they are watching, what they plan to watch, what they finished, and the stories they want to remember.

The app includes anime and manga libraries, ratings, notes, favorites, simple history visuals, and live anime discovery powered by public anime data sources.

AnimeBoxD is built as an independent fan project. It is not affiliated with MyAnimeList, Jikan, anime studios, publishers, or streaming platforms.
